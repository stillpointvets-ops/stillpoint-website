Veteran-founded • Serving those who served

Healing for veterans, beyond the prescription.

Stillpoint Veterans Foundation connects veterans living with PTSD and other mental health challenges to local, holistic care — treatment that helps you heal without relying on long-term medication.

Get Connected Our Story
If you are a veteran in crisis, call the Veterans Crisis Line: dial 988, then press 1 (or text 838255). Help is available 24/7.

You carried the mission. Let us help carry this.

Too many veterans are handed a bottle of pills and sent on their way. We believe there's a better path. Stillpoint helps you find holistic mental health care in your community — and walks alongside you to get there.

★ Founded by a 20-year Army veteran ★ Focused on PTSD & mental wellness ★ Partnered with local providers

How we help

One conversation is all it takes to get started.

🧠

Holistic PTSD support

We focus on treatment methods that address the whole person — mind and body — rather than masking symptoms with sustained medication.

🤝

Local connections

We partner with mental health facilities in the community so you can access real, hands-on care close to home.

💬

A guide who gets it

Stillpoint was founded by a fellow veteran. You'll be understood — no judgment, no runaround.

Getting connected is simple

1

Reach out

Fill out the short form or give us a call. Tell us a little about what you're going through.

2

We listen

We'll talk with you about your needs and the holistic options available near you.

3

Get matched

We connect you with a local partner facility that offers the kind of care that fits you.

Our founder

Built by a soldier, for soldiers.

Stillpoint Veterans Foundation was founded by Jamus Patrick, SFC (Ret.), a 20-year U.S. Army infantry veteran. Having served, he knows the weight that veterans carry home — and he started Stillpoint to make sure fewer of them face it alone.
