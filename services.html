What We Do

Connecting veterans to holistic healing.

We help veterans living with PTSD and other mental health challenges find local care that treats the whole person — without depending on long-term medication.

How Stillpoint helps

🧠

Holistic PTSD care

We focus on holistic treatment methods for mental health — approaches that address the mind and body together, aiming for lasting healing rather than sustained reliance on pharmaceuticals.

🤝

Local partnerships

We partner with mental health facilities in the community to expand access to care and help veterans find the right provider close to home.

📢

Getting the word out

Many veterans simply don't know these options exist. A big part of our work is spreading awareness so more veterans can find a better path forward.

What to expect

Reaching out costs nothing and commits you to nothing.

1

Tell us about you

Send us a message or call. Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with about what you're facing.

2

We talk it through

We'll listen and help you understand the holistic mental health options available in your area.

3

We connect you

We point you to a trusted local partner facility that offers care suited to your needs.

Ready when you are

There's no wrong reason to reach out. Whether you're struggling today or just want to understand your options, we're here.

Get Connected

If you are a veteran in crisis, call the Veterans Crisis Line: dial 988, then press 1 (or text 838255). Help is available 24/7.
