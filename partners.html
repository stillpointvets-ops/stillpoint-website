Health Care Partners

The providers we connect you with.

We partner with trusted local facilities that offer holistic, evidence-based care for veterans living with PTSD and other mental health challenges.

Partner facilities

Ketamine Is Hope

South Bend, Indiana

A South Bend clinic offering IV ketamine therapy, EMDR, and Exomind therapy for PTSD, depression, anxiety, and related conditions — an alternative path for those seeking relief beyond traditional medication.

Sophia Zen Center

South Bend, Indiana

A nonprofit meditation community offering guidance and support for Zen practice in the Maria Kannon and Sanbo Zen traditions. Beginners are welcome and start with a simple orientation — a quiet, grounding practice for veterans seeking stillness and mental clarity.
