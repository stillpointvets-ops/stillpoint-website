# How to Update the Stillpoint Veterans Foundation Website

A plain-English cheat sheet. Keep this file — you can re-read it anytime.

## Your website addresses
- **Real address:** https://stillpointvets.org
- **Backup address (always works):** https://stillpoint-website-gold.vercel.app

Both show the same site.

## The three services that run your site (you rarely touch these)
- **GitHub** — the filing cabinet that stores your website's files.
  Your repository: github.com/stillpointvets-ops/stillpoint-website
- **Vercel** — takes those files and shows them to the world. Free.
- **Formspree** — catches messages from your Contact form and emails them to
  stillpointvets@gmail.com. Free.

## Where your files live on your computer
Inside your **STILLPOINT VETERANS FOUNDATION** folder, in the **website** folder.

## How to change anything (the easy way)
1. Just tell me (Claude) in plain words what you want changed. Examples:
   - "Change my phone number to ___"
   - "Add a sentence to the About page that says ___"
   - "Make the headline say ___ instead"
2. I'll update the files in your **website** folder.
3. To make the change appear on the live site, the updated file needs to go
   back to GitHub. You do this the same way we first uploaded:
   - Go to your GitHub repository (link above).
   - Click **Add file → Upload files**.
   - Drag in the changed file(s) I tell you.
   - Click **Commit changes**.
4. Vercel notices automatically and republishes your site in about **1 minute**.
   Refresh stillpointvets.org and your change is live.

That's it. You never have to type any code or commands.

## How long do changes take to go live?
About **1 minute** after you upload to GitHub. If you don't see it, refresh the
page, or wait another minute.

## Your contact form
- Messages from the Contact page arrive in **stillpointvets@gmail.com**.
- Check your inbox (and spam folder, just in case) for new inquiries.

## If something looks broken
- Try refreshing the page first.
- Then just tell me what you see, in plain words, and I'll fix it.

## Good to know
- The **Veterans Crisis Line** notice (988, then press 1) is built into every page.
- Your 501(c)(3) note currently says "status pending." Tell me when it's approved
  and I'll update it (and we can add a Donate button then, if you want).
