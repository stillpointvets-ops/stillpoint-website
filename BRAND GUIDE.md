# Stillpoint Veterans Foundation — Brand Guide

The official look and feel. Use this for the website, documents, social posts, and anything else.

## Logo
- Primary: full-color logo (blue head + maroon tree, "STILLPOINT / VETERANS FOUNDATION" wordmark).
- On dark backgrounds: use the white "reversed" version.
- One-color use: all-black version.
- Always keep clear space around the logo at least the height of the "S" in STILLPOINT.
- Don't stretch, recolor, or box the logo.

## Colors
- **Primary Blue** — `#3D679A`  (RGB 61, 103, 154)
- **Primary Maroon** — `#A23D63`  (RGB 162, 61, 99)
- **White** — `#FFFFFF`  (background)

How they're used on the website:
- Blue: headings, wordmark, links, step numbers, secondary buttons.
- Maroon: main call-to-action buttons ("Get Connected"), the star accent, and the crisis-line notice.
- White: page background.

## Typography
- **Bebas Neue** — headline / wordmark font (bold, condensed, all-caps). Used for the logo, page headings, and section titles.
- **Montserrat** — supporting font. Used for body text, buttons, navigation, and card titles.

## Tagline words
Healing Minds · Restoring Hope · Building Community

## Notes
- The website loads these fonts automatically from Google Fonts.
- Brand colors are stored once at the top of the website's styles file, so changing a value there updates the whole site.
