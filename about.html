Our Story

Twenty years of service. A lifetime of caring.

Stillpoint exists because the fight for a veteran's well-being shouldn't end when the uniform comes off.

Meet the founder

Stillpoint Veterans Foundation was founded by Jamus Patrick, SFC (Ret.) — a 20-year U.S. Army veteran and infantryman (11B). Over two decades in uniform, he saw firsthand what service asks of the men and women who answer the call, and what too many of them carry home.

After retiring, Jamus set out to do something about it. Stillpoint is his answer: a foundation dedicated to helping fellow veterans find real, lasting relief from PTSD and the mental health challenges that so often follow service.

What we believe

Healing shouldn't mean a lifetime on medication.

🌿

The whole person matters

PTSD affects the mind and the body. We champion holistic treatment methods that help veterans truly heal — not just quiet the symptoms.

🛡

No veteran left to struggle alone

Reaching out for help is hard. We make the next step easier by doing the legwork and connecting you to trusted local care.

🏠

Care close to home

We partner with mental health facilities in the community, so support is never far away.

Our mission

Stillpoint Veterans Foundation connects veterans with local, holistic treatment methods for mental health — with a focus on PTSD — so they can find healing that doesn't depend on the sustained use of pharmaceuticals. By partnering with local mental health providers, we help get the word out and get veterans the care they deserve.

Get Connected

If you are a veteran in crisis, call the Veterans Crisis Line: dial 988, then press 1 (or text 838255). Help is available 24/7.
